About me

I move between code, evidence, and institutions.

I am a technology architect and education policy graduate trained to see both the machinery of a system and the people living inside it.

My background spans enterprise technology, software development, K–12 education, graduate research, program evaluation, and product design. That range helps me ask better questions: What is the process? Where does it break? Who carries the burden? What evidence should guide the decision? What can technology improve without making the system less human?
